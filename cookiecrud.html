Cookie Monster's NomNomNom

Click the cookies and cookie monster will eat them! Add your own cookies too!

I created this as an assignment for UCSD's Full Stack Coding Bootcamp. The assignment was to create a CRUD application where users could create different burgers, and keep track of ones they have eaten vs not eaten. Of course, I changed this to Cookies to amuse my 2 year old son's obsession with Sesame Street.

This is a full stack application. Burgers added are added to a mySQL database, and then displayed in the browser. After a user clicks "Eat this Cookie" a boolean is then changed, and the cookie is displayed on the right side of the screen based on this boolean. For added fun, specific names of cookies will display with different images. Try "animal" or "christmas"-- see if you can guess the other cookie names!
