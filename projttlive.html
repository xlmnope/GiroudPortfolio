TurboTax Live

In 2017 I was a part of the team that launched TurboTax Live. With TurboTax Live, customers can get a review from a CPA/EA before they file. In a very compressed fast-paced 4-week timeline I led a cross-functional team through ideation to execution, resulting in over a million dollars in revenue.

Currently I am working on different ways for customers to receive their 'Expert review', with a goal of increasing NPS, conversion, and reducing CPA/EA average handle time per contact. Through data analysis and qualitative research we identified the areas of highest opportunity, priorities and focous for the upcoming tax year. We utilized lab research and usertesting.com to then explore different concepts, ensuring that we were solving for customer needs. This project now spans multiple business units, functions, and SCRUM teams, requiring a high level of cross-team leadership, collaboration, and strong product owner skills. Balancing business costs, operational, and legal constraints with customer needs has been a challenging but rewarding experience.
